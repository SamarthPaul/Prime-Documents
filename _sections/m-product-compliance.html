PR-LC-001

Product Compliance (Legal Compliance module)

Product-level framework under the Legal Compliance module that tracks every product certification & standard the enterprise’s products hold or need — FSSAI, AGMARK, BIS, organic marks, craft marks, GI, and more. It is the product-scoped companion to Business Registration (which covers entrepreneur / business-level registrations): same card-based engine and intervention bridge, but each certification is tied to a product and to the relevant authority & validity.
Certification Master (product level, grouped). A single dropdown grouped into six buckets — Food & Beverage (FSSAI Basic / State / Central, AGMARK, Spices Board, APEDA, ISO 22000), Organic & Natural (Organic NPOP, India Organic, PGS-India Organic), Craft & Textile (Khadi Mark, Handloom Mark, Handicraft Mark, Silk Mark), Quality & Standards (BIS Certification, ISO 9001), IP & Market (Trademark, Geographical Indication, IEC), Jewellery (Hallmark BIS) — plus Other. The master is config-driven; the same certification cannot be added twice for the same product on Existing.
Intervention bridge (Rule 3). Toggling Need Certification Intervention? ON in an Existing card auto-creates a linked card in Required, carrying the certification, product, validity and justification across; the Existing Type (Renewal / Reapply) maps to the Required Phase (Renewal / New Registration). Bridged Required cards are locked (“↻ Bridged from Existing” marker, read-only justification); removing either side un-links the other (two-way sync).

On-Screen UI Structure

A teal title bar reads Product Compliance with crumb PRIME Rural · Product-level Certifications. A horizontal tab rail — Summary · Existing · Required · Resources · Task Log — sits below it, active tab teal, entrepreneur chip on every panel (Rule 2).

Existing and Required render each certification as a collapsible card (sea-green header with a grouped certification selector + status chip; a “⚠ Intervention Flagged” chip appears when toggled). “+ Add Certification” / “+ Add New Certification” append a card. Each card carries an Applicable Product link, certification details (ID, authority, dates, validity, govt-certified, status, scanned copy) and — on Existing — an intervention block. Summary is read-only (certification-wise overview table, aggregate cards, intervention-flag tiles). Resources and Task Log are editable grids. As in Business Registration, a Lifetime Validity checkbox hides Valid Till, and certifications expiring within 90 days surface amber / expired red in Summary.

Key validations (whole form): Entrepreneur is required and unique (one Product Compliance form per entrepreneur). Every card must have a Certification Type and an Applicable Product selected; the same certification cannot be added twice for the same product on Existing. Issued Date ≤ today; Valid Till > Issued Date and is hidden / cleared when Lifetime Validity is checked. Existing Status is mandatory. Flagging an intervention requires a Justification and creates exactly one bridged Required card; bridged cards are locked and stay two-way synced. Expiring-within-90-days / Expired highlighting is computed. Entrepreneur / business-level registrations belong in Business Registration, not here.
