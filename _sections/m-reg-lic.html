PR-LC-002

Business Registration (Legal Compliance module)

Entrepreneur / business-level framework under the Legal Compliance module (formerly “Registration & Licensing” under Product Development). Records every entrepreneur- and business-level registration the enterprise holds and every one it still needs — legal structure, tax IDs, operating licences, market-access registrations and statutory NOCs. Product-level certifications (FSSAI, BIS, AGMARK, organic marks…) live in the companion Product Compliance framework (same module), not here. An intervention bridge carries any registration flagged in Existing into the Required tab for renewal / re-application.
Registration Master (entrepreneur / business level, grouped). A single dropdown grouped into five buckets — Legal Structure (Sole Proprietorship, Partnership Firm, LLP, Private Limited Company, Producer Company, Cooperative Society, SHG, FPO, Section 8 Company), Identity & Tax (PAN, Aadhaar (Entrepreneur), GST, TAN), Business Operations (Udyam / MSME, Trade License (Municipal), Shop & Establishment, Bank Account), Market Access (GeM, DPIIT Startup Recognition, APMC, NEHHDC, IEC), NOCs & Compliance (Pollution Control NOC, Fire Safety NOC, Health & Trade License) — plus Other. The master is config-driven; the same registration cannot be added twice on the Existing tab.
Intervention bridge (Rule 3). Toggling Need Registration / Licensing Intervention? ON in an Existing card auto-creates a linked card in Required, carrying the registration name, validity and justification across. The Existing Type (Renewal / Reapply) maps to the Required Phase (Renewal / New Registration). Bridged Required cards are locked (registration selector disabled, “↻ Bridged from Existing” marker, read-only justification); removing either side un-links the other (two-way sync).

On-Screen UI Structure

A teal title bar reads Business Registration with crumb PRIME Rural · Entrepreneur & Business-level Registrations. A horizontal tab rail — Summary · Existing · Required · Resources · Task Log — sits below it, active tab highlighted teal, with an entrepreneur chip (“Auto-fetched from profile”) on every panel (Rule 2).

Existing and Required render each registration as a collapsible card (sea-green header carrying a registration-selector dropdown + status chip; a “⚠ Intervention Flagged” chip appears when toggled). “+ Add Registration” / “+ Add New Registration” append a card. Each Existing card holds a Registration Details subsection and a terracotta Intervention Need block; each Required card holds the same details plus a Phase field and a Status block. Summary is read-only (overview table + four aggregate cards + four intervention-flag tiles). Resources and Task Log are editable child-table grids.

Validity-aware highlighting: a Lifetime Validity checkbox hides the Valid Till date; registrations expiring within 90 days surface amber (“⏰”), expired ones red, in the Summary overview.

Key validations (whole form): Entrepreneur is required and unique (one Business Registration form per entrepreneur). Every card must have a Registration Type selected and the same registration cannot be added twice on Existing. Issued Date ≤ today; Valid Till > Issued Date and is hidden / cleared when Lifetime Validity is checked. Existing Status is mandatory. Toggling the intervention ON requires a Justification and creates exactly one bridged Required card; bridged cards are locked (selector disabled, justification read-only) and stay two-way synced — removing either side un-links the other. Expiring-within-90-days and Expired highlighting are computed, never stored. Product-level certifications belong in Product Compliance, not here.
