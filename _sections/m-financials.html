PR-AC-001

Financials

The Financials framework is the Accounts module of the Diagnostic Framework. It captures the entrepreneur's baseline financial picture (investment sources, loans, revenue & expenditure), bookkeeping practice pre/post intervention, monthly performance over time, and a Business Model Calculator that derives monthly break-even. Golden-Thread ties: the Calculator auto-fetches Existing loans from Financial Assessment, Existing revenue/expense from the Revenue & Expenditure card, and total monthly production quantity from the Production Capacity framework; its Projected break-even feeds the Summary tab.
Key behaviour: data flows across tabs — Financial Assessment (Loans) → Calculator (Existing Loan 1 & 2); Financial Assessment (Revenue & Expenditure) → Calculator (Existing column, editable override); Growth (monthly table) → Summary KPIs & trend chart; Calculator (Projected break-even) → Summary Monthly Break-even card. Bookkeeping Q1–Q3 captured Pre (Financial Assessment) and Post (Growth) drive the Summary Growth Trajectory.

On-Screen UI Structure

A teal hero bar reads "Financials" with the subtitle "Accounts · Financial Bookkeeping · Applicable to Entrepreneur" and Back / Save buttons. Below sits a horizontal tab rail of five tabs: Summary, Financial Assessment, Growth, Business Model Calculator, and Task Log.

Summary (virtual / read-only, rendered by DfFinancialsSummary.vue) leads with a 4-tile KPI grid (Total Investment, Avg Monthly Revenue, Avg Net Profit/Month, Profit Margin), then a two-column split of an "Investment & Loans" card (investment breakdown bar list + loans table) and a "Revenue & Income" averages card. Below is an SVG "Monthly Revenue & Profit Trend" bar chart (teal=revenue, terracotta=profit, redraws live from Growth edits), a "Bookkeeping Growth Trajectory" card of three Pre→Post comparison tiles (Practices Bookkeeping, Bookkeeping Level, Maintenance Frequency), and a "Monthly Break-even" card of two teal/terracotta hero tiles fetched from the Calculator's Projected scenario.

Financial Assessment opens with a Details card, then Section I — Assessment (Investment Sources child table, Loan Details child table, Revenue & Expenditure field grid with calculated profit/overall/margin), then Section II — Bookkeeping Practice (three baseline questions Q1–Q3) followed by a terracotta Intervention Need block with a toggle + justification textarea.

Growth has Section I — Monthly Tracker (a monthly performance child table with totals + three teal average stat tiles) and Section II — Implementation Status (terracotta), the post-intervention bookkeeping reassessment Q1–Q3.

Business Model Calculator is a collapsible four-section calculator (I. Loan Terms grid with auto-fetched/manual rows + add-to-total checkboxes; II. Revenue & Expense with Existing/Projected columns; III. Monthly Break-even output table; IV. Capital Costs card), ending in a teal result hero, a status banner (good/warn/bad), and a help card. A legend distinguishes auto-fetched (read-only), input, and auto-calculated cells.

Task Log is a single child table of bookkeeping intervention tasks with date, task dropdown, notes, and an upload/view attachment cell.

Calculated fields render italic on a cream-deep background with a formula tooltip (General Rule 1); auto-fetched fields are read-only on cream-deep (Rule 2).
