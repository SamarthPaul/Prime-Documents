PR-LG-001

Logistic Packing

Product-level framework (Logistics module) that records the outer / transit packing used to ship each product — box type, material, dimensions, transport mode, load & stacking limits, damage history and per-unit cost — together with a months-as-rows monthly shipment-quantity grid. Distinct from Product Packaging (which covers primary consumer packaging). An intervention bridge carries flagged packing into a Required tab for a recommended replacement spec.
Golden Thread & pattern. Mirrors Product Packaging: products are added as collapsible blocks (Product Name + SKU), each holding Section I packing-detail rows and Section III intervention need; a shared Section II Monthly Shipment Quantities grid (months ↓ rows, each product’s packing type → column) spans cohort years (Rule 11) and computes annual transit-packing cost (Σ month qty × price/unit). Toggling Packing Intervention Needed? auto-creates a linked row in Required Section I (bridge, Rule 3); removing it clears the flag (two-way).

On-Screen UI Structure

A teal title bar reads Logistic Packing; a horizontal tab rail — Summary · Existing · Required · Resources · Task Log · Log Book — sits below, active tab teal, with the entrepreneur chip on every panel (Rule 2).

Existing renders each product as a collapsible block (sea-green header with Product Name + SKU). Section I is a repeating packing-detail grid (4-column cells per row) capturing type, material, source, dimensions, capacity, price, transport mode, load/stacking limits, damage observed, picture, a four-pill Properties group (Fragile Suitable / Reusable / Waterproof / Cold Chain) and a note. Section III is a terracotta Intervention Need block per packing type. The shared Section II Monthly Shipment Quantities grid sits below all blocks.

Required holds Section I Recommended Replacement Specification (bridge-populated), Section II New Packing (standalone) and Section III Monthly Shipment Quantities — Required. Summary is read-only (overview table with a Damage Risk column, aggregate cards, intervention-flag tiles incl. Damage Risk Flagged & Reusable Packing). Resources, Task Log and Log Book close the rail. Calculated fields render italic on cream-deep with formula tooltips (Rule 1).

Section II — New Packing (standalone)

Same packing field set as Section I, added freely for transit packing not linked to any existing product/type, with its own Implementation Status (Pending / In Progress / Completed / On Hold).

Section III — Monthly Shipment Quantities (Required)

Mirrors the Existing monthly grid for the required side (months ↓ rows, required packing types → columns); Month Total = Σ(qty × required price/unit). Spans cohort years (Rule 11).

Key validations (whole form): Entrepreneur is required and unique (one Logistic Packing form per entrepreneur). Each product block needs a name and at least one packing row; Type of Packing is mandatory on every row (Existing & Required). Capacity, Price/Unit, Max Load, Quantity Shipped must be ≥ 0; Stacking Layers ≥ 1. Calculated fields (Month Total, Annual qty/type, Annual Cost, Avg Monthly Qty, Saving) are read-only with formula tooltips (Rule 1) and the monthly grid spans cohort years (Rule 11). Toggling an intervention ON creates exactly one bridged Required replacement row; removing it clears the flag (two-way sync). Distinct from Product Packaging — this framework records outer / transit packing only.
