PR-FN-002

Schemes & Funding

Entrepreneur-level framework (Funding module) that tracks every government scheme, grant and loan the entrepreneur has accessed or should pursue — eligibility, application status, disbursed amounts and full loan-repayment / EMI details. Schemes flagged for follow-up bridge into the Required tab; loan repayment figures conceptually align with the Financials framework.
Scheme Master (config-driven, 19 + Other). Includes PMEGP, MUDRA (Shishu / Kishore / Tarun), Stand-Up India, Startup India Seed Fund, MOVCDNER, NABARD (Capital Investment Subsidy / Rural Innovation Fund), KVIB Loan, DIC Loan, CMEGP Meghalaya, MBMA Livelihood Programme, SELCO Foundation Grant, LIFCOM Loan, NEHHDC Marketing Support, MSME Champions, CGTMSE, NSIC Single Point Registration, and Other.
Intervention bridge (Rule 3) & EMI calc. Toggling the per-scheme Intervention block ON auto-creates a row in Required → Section I (carrying scheme + justification); Section II is for genuinely new applications. EMI / Month is computed (flat) as Loan Amount ÷ Loan Term (months) and is read-only. Status options: Applied / Sanctioned / Disbursed / Rejected / Under Review; Eligibility: Eligible / Not Eligible / Document Pending.

On-Screen UI Structure

A teal title bar reads Schemes & Funding; a horizontal tab rail — Summary · Existing · Required · Resources · Task · Log Book — sits below, active tab teal, entrepreneur chip on every panel (Rule 2).

Existing renders each scheme as a row/card with three subsections: (a) Scheme & Eligibility, (b) Application & Amounts (status, dates, disbursed & loan amount), and (c) Loan Repayment Details (loan no., amount, interest, outstanding, term, EMI/month, start/end dates, contribution %), plus a terracotta Intervention block. Required has Section I Flagged (bridge-populated) and Section II New scheme applications. Summary is read-only: Existing vs Required stat cards (count, disbursed ₹, total EMI/month) plus an Existing status-distribution strip (Applied / Sanctioned / Disbursed / Rejected / Under Review). Resources (Scheme Master List, MUDRA reference, Loan Documentation Checklist), Task and Log Book close the rail. Computed fields (EMI, outstanding, totals) render italic on cream-deep with formula tooltips (Rule 1).

Key validations (whole form): Entrepreneur is required and unique (one Schemes & Funding form per entrepreneur). Each Existing row needs a Scheme selected; Disbursed Amount, Loan Amount, Outstanding and Interest must be ≥ 0; Loan Term ≥ 1 (months). EMI/Month (= Loan Amount ÷ Term) and the disbursed / EMI totals are read-only with formula tooltips (Rule 1). Disbursement Date ≥ Application Date; Loan End > Start. Flagging an Intervention creates exactly one Required Section I row and stays two-way synced. Loan repayment figures align with the Financials framework where present.
